Kat Cammack’s Veterans Town Hall, Friday, July 8

U.S. Representative Kat Cammack scheduled a veterans town hall just days after she voted no on a war powers resolution that called for an end to the unjust Iran War. Representative Cammack also voted for the One Big Beautiful Bill which harms veterans who are receiving SNAP benefits and initially failed to support the PACT Act that expanded health benefits for veterans 

![](/uploads/20260710_171736.jpg)

exposed to burn pits and other toxic substances during their service. Her message to veterans has become clear, she will support a war with no clear strategy that is costing billions of dollar a day without hesitation but not so the costs of veteran health care. To call out this injustice, members of Nature Coast Indivisible gathered with  members of Gainesville Veterans for Peace, Citrus Solidarity and Ocala Blue Dots outside the town hall.

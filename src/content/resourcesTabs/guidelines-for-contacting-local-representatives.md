When contacting elected officials, clear and respectful messages are most effective.

### Suggested structure

1. Introduce yourself and your city/county.
2. State the issue and your requested action.
3. Share one short, concrete reason this issue matters to your community.
4. Request a reply.

You can also include links to supporting sources and local impact stories.
